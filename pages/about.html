About

The Zenith Classes initially known as Zenith Guru Coaching Center was started in 2013 by Vinayak Rastogi and Raahim Ali. It was started as a passion and became an emotion, helping 1000s of students to achieve their goals. During Pandemic Zenith Guru Coaching Center started online Tutoring and then know by the alias 'Zenith Classes'. Coming to online platform was a big success. More Students were able to get quality education from all over India.Our main motive is to provide high level education in least price possible

Goals

To provide everyone with high quality of education in the least price possible. Today Zenith classes is known by students all over India. Now we have opened our headquarters in Pryagraj for offline classes, where students can interact with teachers more often

Features

Our programmers have worked really hard to make our online lecture system effective, so teachers and students encounter less problems for learning effectively.

We provide students Daily Practice Paper (DPP) which helps them to make a better understanding of the topic.

After every class there's a small doubt clearing session to make sure no student get left behind.

We have teachers who can teach in different language to break the language barrier between Students

Faculty

Why students only deserve a admission test, why not teachers ? We make sure that the teacher is experienced in his field of education and the ways he teaches is unique and is understandable by students.

Now we have over 2100+ teachers which excel in their field of education. We started as a team of 2 and now a Nation wide Coaching Institute teaching students from different places
